# The NexSocio Manifesto

**One app. Three lanes. Your whole life — without the blur.**

---

## We believe

You are not one feed.

You are a friend on Saturday, a seller on Monday, and a voice for your company on Tuesday. Those are different selves. They deserve different spaces — not different passwords, not different apps, not one chaotic timeline where your boss sees your weekend and your customers see your memes.

**We believe social should respect context.**

Connection should not cost coherence. Creating should not require a dozen platforms. Commerce should not be an afterthought. Safety should not be invisible. And the world should not need another app that mixes everything together and calls it convenience.

---

## What NexSocio is

**NexSocio** is an integrated socio-economic platform: social network, professional network, marketplace, and collaboration — built as one multilingual home, with walls between worlds that should never collide.

- **Nex** — connection, the link between people, places, and purpose  
- **Socio** — society, the communities and economies we build together  

We are not trying to be the loudest feed on earth. We are building the **right** home for the many roles you already live.

---

## Three lanes. Never mixed.

Every post on NexSocio lives in exactly one lane:

| Lane | For | Never shows up in |
|------|-----|-------------------|
| **Personal** | Friends, reels, live, creativity, real life | Business or corporate feeds |
| **Business** | Your trade, shop, promos, marketplace | Personal or corporate feeds |
| **Corporate** | Company updates, careers, LinkedIn-style voice | Personal or business feeds |

Same identity. Same wallet. Same inbox. **Separate worlds.**

That separation is not a setting. It is the architecture of trust.

## What you can do here

**Create** — TikTok-style filters, studio, reels, live streams. Your look. Your voice. Your audience.

**Earn** — NexCoins, gifts, qualified views, creator payouts. Monetisation that lives beside the content, not three apps away.

**Sell** — Listings, cart, wallet checkout, QR shop links. Turn community into commerce without leaving the lane.

**Connect** — Voice and video calls, meetings, teams, status, contacts. Real-time presence, not a link to another product.

**Represent** — Digital twins and talking avatars for when you are busy but still want to show up — safely, on your terms.

**Find & protect** — Location tools, Find Me, moderation, kids flows, age-aware modes. Social with guardrails, not guilt.

**Speak globally** — Twenty languages. RTL where it matters. A PWA that works where you are.

## Corporate lane policy

Corporate is not open signup with a personal Gmail. It is **verified B2B**:

1. **Corporate email only** — organisations register with a company-domain email. Personal providers are rejected.
2. **Credentials before selling** — company registration, licence body (Companies House, SRA, CQC, etc.), and sector category must be submitted and approved before advertising services to the public.
3. **Public services, private network** — everyone can **see** verified corporate services (health, legal, education, sports, faith, accounting, and more). **Networking** (people, jobs, education posts, wellbeing programmes) requires a **corporate subscription**.
4. **Free trial, then paid** — qualifying organisations get **30 days free** networking. After trial: paid subscription for serious B2B networking, job posts, and sector communities.

**Sectors include:** Health & Care · Wellbeing · Legal · Accounting · Education · Sports · Faith & Community · Technology · Non-profit · Professional Services.
